about

👋 Hey, I'm Mark — dad of three (that's the best bit).

I've spent a lifetime chasing what's just over the next hill — from 100-mile ultras, IM 🏃‍♂️🚴‍♂️ and a sub 3 marathon to exploring 90+ countries 🌍, visiting everywhere from Yemen to Japan to Syria to Panama. Always curious, always moving.

A few years ago, I dove into startup life as a non-technical founder. Built a feature-rich app with a small team and big dreams. It didn't land — and honestly, it drained me. Financially, emotionally, the whole lot..

So I hit reset. Learned to code using AI 🤖 with the help of a Scriba course and Cursor as my playground. I've since built a few small tools I needed — and I'm sharing them here in case they help you too 🛠️.

I'm inspired by laptop builders like Pieter Levels, Jack Friks & Marc Lou — drawn to the freedom, ownership, and quiet confidence of shipping simple, useful things.

When I'm not vibe coding, you'll find me training for Hyrox 💪, chasing a PR, or doing LEGO with the kids 🧱. Probably in that order.

👇 Check out what I've built — and feel free to say hey on X.
