About

👋 Mark here -- dad-in-training, code-curious, always trying to move forward.

I've run 100-mile ultras, tackled Ironman, gone sub-3 in the marathon 🏃‍♂️🚴‍♂️ - and wandered far-flung places 🌍 from Yemen to Japan to Nicaragua. Always curious, always moving forward.

After a swing at startup life left me frustrated, I hit reset. Learned to code with AI 🤖, and now I ship products I need — maybe you do too 🛠️.

Inspired by quiet builders and laptop freedom: Levels, Friks, and Lou 💻.

When I'm not vibe coding, I'm training for Hyrox 💪 or knee-deep in a LEGO mission with the kids 🧱.
