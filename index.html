about

👋 Mark here -- dad-in-training, code-curious, always trying to move forward.

I've run 100-mile ultras, tackled Ironman, gone sub-3 in the marathon 🏃‍♂️🚴‍♂️ - and wandered far-flung places 🌍 from Yemen to Japan to Nicaragua. Always curious, always moving forward.
