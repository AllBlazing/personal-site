about

👋 Hey, I'm Mark — dad of three (that's the best bit).

I've spent a lifetime chasing what's just over the next hill — from 100-mile ultras, IM 🏃‍♂️🚴‍♂️ and a sub 3 marathon to exploring 90+ countries 🌍, from Yemen to Japan to Syria. Always curious, always moving.

After a draining startup journey as a non-technical founder, I hit reset. Learned to code using AI 🤖 with Scriba and Cursor. Now I build simple, useful tools — sharing them here in case they help you too 🛠️.

Inspired by laptop builders like Pieter Levels, Jack Friks & Marc Lou — drawn to the freedom and quiet confidence of shipping useful things.

When I'm not coding, I'm training for Hyrox 💪, chasing PRs, or doing LEGO with the kids 🧱.

👇 Check out what I've built — and feel free to say hey on X.
